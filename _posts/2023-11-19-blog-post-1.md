2017年台灣的物理奧林匹亞決選理論模擬試題中出現了一題較為困難的題目，其要求學生求解給定特定初值條件與邊界條件時彈性棒中的縱波。原解使用一維波動的達朗貝爾解，並用邊界條件將彈性棒假想的延伸，進而求解。此解法雖然數學較為初等卻需要較抽象的思考，並非所有選手都能馬上理解。筆者今欲以傅立葉級數的方式重解此題供讀者參考。

In the 2017 Taiwanese physics olympiad team selection test, there was a relatively difficult problem on logitudal waves. The official solution employed the d'Alembert solution, which makes the solution elementary but abstract. Herein I present an alternative approach using Fourier series. 

題目敘述 Description 
======

一根理想彈性棒，長 $L$、密度 $\rho$、截面積 $A$、楊氏係數 $Y$，垂直懸掛於一個平面下。在時間 $t=0$ 時突然將此彈性棒放開使其自由下落，定義 $x$ 為彈性體上由上往下指的體座標，求彈性體的位移 $\xi(x,t)$。

Consider an ideal elastic rod with length $L$, density $\rho$, cross sectional area $A$, Young's module $Y$, initially hanging vertically from a surface. At time $t= 0$, drop the rod so that it starts free falling. Let $x$ be the body coordinate on the elastic rod pointing downwards, find the displacement of the rod $\xi(x,t)$.   

解 Solution 
======

波動方程式之通解 General solution of the wave equation
------
首先列出波動方程式

First, write down the wave equaiton 

$$\rho \frac{\partial^2\xi}{\partial t^2}=Y\frac{\partial^2\xi}{\partial x^2}+\rho g$$

和邊界條件

and the boundary conditions 

$$\frac{\partial \xi(0,t)}{\partial x}=\frac{\partial \xi(L,t)}{\partial x}=0 \; ,\; \frac{\partial \xi(0,0)}{\partial t}=0$$

可以假設這個解族為

the family of solutions is 

$$\xi(x,t)=\frac{1}{2}gt^2 +\frac{1}{2}a_0 + \sum_{n=1}^\infty \left[a_n \cos(\frac{n\pi}{L}x)+b_n \sin(\frac{n\pi}{L}x)\right]\left[A_n \cos(\omega_n t)+B_n \sin(\omega_n t )\right]$$

其中，$\displaystyle c=\sqrt{\frac{Y}{\rho}}$，$\displaystyle  \omega_n = \frac{n\pi c}{L}$，將解帶入邊界條件簡化可得

where $\displaystyle c=\sqrt{\frac{Y}{\rho}}$，$\displaystyle  \omega_n = \frac{n\pi c}{L}$, using these parameters and the boundary conditions the general solution can be simplified 

$$\xi(x,t)=\frac{1}{2}gt^2 +\frac{1}{2}a_0 + \sum_{n=1}^\infty a_n \cos(\frac{n\pi}{L}x) \cos(\omega_n t )$$

這就是此方程的一般解。

which is the general solution to the equation.

求解初值條件 Finding initial conditions
------

再來要解出靜止懸掛的情況作為初值條件。將波動方程的時間項忽略可得

Then, we find the initial conditions of the hangin elastic rod by ignoring the time dependent component of the wave equation

$$\xi_{xx}(x,0)=-\frac{\rho g}{Y}$$

積分一次，並帶入 $\xi_x (L,t)=0$，可得

by integrating and subbing in $\xi_x (L,t)=0$ we have

$$\xi_x (x,0)=\frac{\rho g}{Y}(L-x)$$

再積分一次並帶入 $\xi(0,0)=0$，解得

integrate once more and sub in $\xi(0,0)=0$ we have

$$\xi(x,0)=\frac{\rho g}{Y}\left(Lx -\frac{1}{2}x^2\right)$$

解傅立葉係數 Solve for the Fourier coefficients
------

根據前一小節獲得的初值條件，有

Sub in initial conditions to the general solution 

$$\xi(x,0)=\frac{1}{2}a_0 + \sum_{n=1}^\infty a_n \cos(\frac{n\pi}{L}x)=\frac{\rho g}{Y}\left(Lx -\frac{1}{2}x^2\right)$$

可解係數

thus the coefficients are

$$a_n =\frac{2}{L}\int_0^L \frac{\rho g}{Y}\left(Lx -\frac{1}{2}x^2 \right)\cos(\frac{n\pi}{L}x) \mathrm{d} x$$

帶入積分公式

Using the integral formula

$$\int_0^\pi \theta \cos(n\theta) \mathrm{d} \theta =-\frac{2}{n^2}\;\text{($n$ odd)}/=0\;\text{($n$ even)}$$ 

$$\int_0^\pi \theta^2 \cos(n\theta) \mathrm{d} \theta =-\frac{2\pi}{n^2}\;\text{($n$ odd)}/=\frac{2\pi}{n^2}\;\text{($n$ even)} $$

並做適當變數變換（留給讀者練習）可得係數

and with suitable change of variables, we obtain

$$a_0 = \frac{2\rho g L^2}{3Y}\;,\;a_n =-\frac{2\rho g L^2}{\pi^2 Y n^2}\; \text{for}\;n\in \mathbb{Z}^+$$

求出特解 The specific solution
------

此方程之完整特解為

The complete specific solution is thus given by

$$\xi(x,t)=\frac{1}{2}gt^2 +\frac{\rho g L^2}{Y}\left(\frac{1}{3}-\frac{2}{\pi^2}\sum_{n=1}^\infty     \frac{1}{n^2} \cos(\frac{n\pi}{L}x) \cos(\frac{n\pi c}{L} t )\right)\;,\;x\in[0,L]\;,\;t\in [0,\infty)$$
